DAY 1 : ARRIVAL HA NOI

Breakfast at hotel. Visit Royal Citadel and Royal Museum. Then, visit Tu Duc, Khai Dinh Mausoleums the best- reserved early 19th and early 20th centuries monuments of the Nguyen emperors to enjoy very different styles of the royal architecture under this Dynasty.
